Controls:

CTRL key: Jump
Left key: Move left
Right key: Move right
Space bar: Blow bubble

Cheat codes (keys):

P key: Pauses/unpauses the game
E key: Show/hide the edges connecting the nodes
1 key: Skip to level 1
2 key: Skip to level 2
L key: Adds extra lives
